Research Interests

My research fields are data science, network science and business analytics. I am interested in developing analytical and practical tools for understanding social and economic networks. I have been engaged in collaborative projects and consulting and training services related to data analytics with industry partners. More information of my publications can be found at my Google Scholar and ResearchGate profiles. My current projects include:

  • Global Production Networks, where network science metrics and techniques such as centrality measures and community detection are applied to international trade and input-output data to gain insights into the dynamics of global value chains and the effects of trade-related policies.

  • Social Media Marketing, where data science methods such as machine learning are applied to data from social media platforms such as Twitter and Weibo to understand the effectiveness of social media marketing.
